Transforming care

A world-class local hospital will benefit local people.

Transformed clinical facilities, pathways, and access to the latest clinical R&D will improve mental healthcare and outcomes for our patients:

  • reducing length of stay and admissions
  • delivering more personalised care
  • support to improve welbeing and quality of life
  • reducing readmissions
