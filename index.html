A bold vision

Together we will create a world-class centre for mental health and brain sciences - combining the strengths of a globally renowned university, one of the UK's leading mental health trusts, and one of the world's outstanding centres for biomedical research.

Transforming research

This centre will drive a new generation of research into the brain and treatments for mental health. We will link the fundamental discoveries happening in brain science to the development of new clinical practices, and do this faster than ever before.

Global reach

It will be a place of global significance. The world faces substantial and growing mental illness. Oxford has unique capabilities right across brain sciences: this centre will harness these to improve treatments for mental health everywhere.

Nurturing talent

We will attract and grow the world's best clinicians, scientists, and innovators, helping them to work across boundaries. This will create a pipeline of talent and new ideas to benefit the NHS, drive growth of high-value jobs, businesses and the UK economy.

Local excellence

A world-class local hospital will benefit local people: Transformed clinical facilities, pathways, and access to the latest clinical R&D will improve mental healthcare and outcomes for our patients - reducing length of stay and admissions; delivering more personalised care and support to improve well-being and quality of life; and reducing readmissions.
