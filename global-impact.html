Harnessing expertise

Warneford Park will be a place of global significance. The world faces substantial and growing mental illness.

Oxford has unique capabilities right across brain sciences: this centre will harness these to improve treatments for mental health everywhere.

Attracting the best

We will attract and grow the world's best clinicians, scientists, and innovators, helping them to work across boundaries.

This will create a pipeline of talent and new ideas to benefit the NHS, drive growth of high-value jobs, businesses and the UK economy.
